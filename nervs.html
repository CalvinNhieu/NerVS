Ludum Dare 29 (2014) Submission: NerVS
Theme: Beneath the Surface
Interpretation: Beneath the Skin - the body's Nervous System
You are responsible for carrying out the electrical signals from the nerve endings (bottom) to the brain (top). Unattended signals will harm your well-being resulting in termination.

HOW TO PLAY:
Clear signals on paths by inputting a path sequence using [W] [A] [D] keys. Use [SPACEBAR] to commit a sequence. Traverse nerve endings using [V] and [B].

HINTS
Small pushes forward are the key...
